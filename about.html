About NYSL

Mission

To support young athlets living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, specifically those skills aroud team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful athletes through the building the character, self-dictiplin, and leadership.

General Information

Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to privide athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside ina Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSl is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous leaugue members.
